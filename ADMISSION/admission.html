UNDERGRADUATE ADMISSION PROCESS

JEE-MAINS

JANUARY-APRIL

Ist PHASE

Admission to BIT Sindri is based on JEE Mains scores. The Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB) uses these scores for the merit list preparation. Your All India Rank (AIR) in JEE Mains determines your chances of securing a seat at BIT Sindri.

JCECEB REGISTRATION

MAY-JUNE

IInd PHASE

To register on the JCECEB portal, students must create an account, provide personal and academic details (including JEE Mains credentials), upload necessary documents, and pay the counseling fee. Afterward, they fill and lock their preferred colleges and branches, such as BIT Sindri. Finally, they download the confirmation slip and await seat allotment results.

RELEASE OF MERIT LIST

JULY-AUGUST

IIIrd PHASE

The merit list is released by JCECEB based on JEE Mains scores and category-wise eligibility. It determines the rank and priority for seat allotment in colleges like BIT Sindri. Students can check the list on the official portal and proceed with counseling accordingly.

COUNSELLING

AUGUST-SEPTEMBER

IVth PHASE

In the counseling process, students register on the JCECEB portal, verify documents, and fill in their preferences for colleges and branches. Seats are allotted based on merit, preferences, and category. After paying the admission fee and confirming the seat, students report to the allotted college to complete formalities.
